Information Required on property tax and user charges for Ministry of Finance scheme

Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs is delighted to launch the cityfinance portal to collect information from states on property tax and user charges. Online submission of information from you would help us in analyzing the information quickly on real time basis.

States are required to undertake following reforms as prerequisites for additional borrowing limit of 0.25 percent of GSDP for the year 2020-21:

(1) Notify (a) floor rates of property tax in ULBs which are in consonance with the prevailing circle rates (i.e. guideline rates for property transactions) and (b) floor rates of user charges in respect of the provision of water-supply, drainage and sewerage which reflect current costs/past inflation

(2) Put in place a system of periodic increase in floor rates of property tax/user charges in line with the price increases.

The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, nodal ministry to assess the progress of these reforms and recommend release of additional borrowing, is collecting the information on existing status and proposed implementation plan for above reforms. It would help us if you answer all the questions in next sections. This online submission of information would be first step towards implementing the scheme.
